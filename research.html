Research

Research themes and ongoing directions.

This page is intended for detailed descriptions of the lab’s major themes, representative projects, and research philosophy.

01

Computational Mechanics

The lab studies numerical modeling, simulation frameworks, and computational techniques for complex physical and engineering systems. This section can later include representative methods, benchmarks, or open-source tools developed by the group.

02

Learning-Enabled Analysis

We explore how machine learning can support analysis, prediction, and workflow acceleration while maintaining scientific interpretability and methodological rigor.

03

Experimental and Applied Systems

The lab translates ideas into practical systems, prototypes, and applied studies. This includes student-led implementations, experimental platforms, and interdisciplinary collaborations.
